<people_hands_and_faces>
A visible face is welcome and often stronger — but ONLY when it can read as a REAL, cleanly photographed

- FACES: fine to show, and you do NOT have to hide or turn them away. Frame them like a real editorial

- HANDS are the weak point: AVOID close-up hands and fingers and never make fingers the subject. Keep
